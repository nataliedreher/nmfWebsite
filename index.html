Hi there! I`m N.M. your friendly neighborhood MM romance author. I`m just a quirky, nerdy girl who finds it easier to speak through the mouths of fictional men than to navigate small talk in the real world. In real life, I often bumble through conversation using song lyrics and movie quotes.

Before I was a novelist, I was a poet and that love of language shines through every page I write. My stories are steeped in emotion and imagery with characters who feel like real people you might pass on the street (and secretly wish you could follow home).

The men you`re most often going to see from my books are tender, if sometimes messy, but protective and soft where it counts. They`re wholesome sweethearts with gentle souls and hidden scars.

When I`m not writing slow-burn romance or arguing with my characters at 2 a.m. because they`ve hijacked the plot, you can find me reading, adventuring, competing, traveling, or passionately yelling at a sports game. I survive on tea and thrive on anything outdoorsy.

Let`s fall in love, one chapter at a time.

~Ezra~

When bartender Ezra first meets Micah under the pulsing lights of a Seattle nightclub, it feels like gravity shifts. Their chemistry is electric, but just as quickly, Micah disappears into the night, leaving Ezra with nothing but the memory of a night that never got to be more. Four months later, fate brings them back together, and Ezra takes the chance he never thought he'd get.

As their second chance transforms, Ezra finds himself drawn deeper, becoming a grounding force in a reality he doesn`t fully understand but is determined to navigate.

~Micah~

Micah has spent years believing he`s too much—too chaotic, too broken—for love. But Ezra sees past the walls he`s built, offering patience, steadiness, and affection he isn`t used to and still questions that he`s worthy of. As their bond deepens, Micah dares to hope for a future where he isn` left behind.

But when the weight of reality catches up to them they both must decide if love is enough to hold them through the storm.

Be Your Gravity is a tender, low-angst, hurt/comfort MM contemporary romance told in alternating perspectives. It's a story about unconditional love, acceptance, and finding beauty in the messy, complicated places we`d rather hide and realizing we`re still worthy of being loved there. Along the way, expect bantering friends, a healthy dose of whimsy, terrible puns, and the kind of intimacy that makes space for both laughter and tears. An HEA is guaranteed.
